TwoHearts Updates of the Week

Thanks for joining us for another week of TwoHearts updates! This week, Metro Boomin's highly anticipated album, HEROS & VILLIANS, was released on December 2. HEROS & VILLAINS is the follow-up to Metro's 2018 debut studio album, NOT ALL HEROS WEAR CAPES. Containing 15 songs, HEROS & VILLAINS is filled with features from Future, 21 Savage, Travis Scott, Chris Brown, The Weeknd, A$AP Rocky, Don Toliver, Jon Legend, Young Nudy, Young Thug, and Gunna. The album also includes narration from Morgan Freeman, who has previously worked with Metro Boomin on his collaboration album with 21 Savage called Savage Mode II, and JayZ.


For any and all Smino and JID fans, don't forget, the JID+Smino Luv is 4 Ever Tour begins on January 22, 2023 in Seattle, WA. Presale opened during October, so if you're still hoping to get tickets, don't wait too long! The US tour ends on March 29 in Nashville, TN. The being said, Smino's Louphoria tour in Europe starts on April 16, 2023 in Copenhagen, Denmark. The final locations and dates for the Euro tour are yet to be announced, so if you're interested, don't forget to follow Smino on Twitter to get updates!
